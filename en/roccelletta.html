Roccelletta

Discover the charm of time standing still.

A journey through time: history, nature and culture on the Ionian coast

Discover one of Calabria's hidden gems: a charming coastal destination in the municipality of Borgia, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and extraordinary archaeological heritage. A place where unspoiled nature blends seamlessly with over two thousand years of history.

We invite you to visit the National Archaeological Park of Scolacium, nestled among centuries-old olive groves. This unique site offers a remarkable journey through time, showcasing the rich historical and cultural legacy from Magna Graecia to the Middle Ages.

Within the park, you can explore the ruins of the ancient city of Skylletion, originally founded by the Greeks and later transformed into a thriving Roman colony. It quickly became an important hub for trade and cultural exchange. Among the highlights are the evocative remains of the Roman Forum and other impressive imperial structures.

The visit continues at the Scolacium Archaeological Museum, which houses a remarkable collection of artifacts: statues, Roman busts and portraits, and a colossal bronze forearm — all offering valuable insight into everyday life and ancient art.

The tour ends with a captivating industrial archaeology section that tells the story of the region’s olive oil tradition. One of the key stops is the Mazza Olive Press, built in 1934, where you can still see the original machinery used for oil production.

Opening hours: every day, from 09:00 AM to 07:00 PM. No reservation is required.
